Understanding Neuroplasticity and its benefits

Understanding neuroplasticity can help you to change unwanted habits and develop new ones. This happens in three key ways: chemical change, structural change, and functional Change.

Checmical chnage occurs in the initial stages of learning something new. Chemical change primarily influences short-term memory or short-term improvement in a motor skill.

Structural change occurs when nuerons in the brain change their connections, altering the brains structure. This degree of change requires more time & effort. Structural change constitutes long-term improvement of memory and a motor skill.

Functional change occurs when entire brain net works change. These brain networks, as they are used over & over again, become more excitable & effecient when activated.

How might you increase Neuroplasticity

There are lot of ways to increase nueroplasticity, some specific and some more general, such as, repetition of a skill, phsyical exercixe, learning new skills, rest, meditation, stimulation, new experiences.

What's a growth mindset?

A person with a growth mindset chooses to engage with an error and learn from it. As opposed to someone with a fixed mindset who runs from an error.
